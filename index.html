Canon Ixus 70

Digital cameras from the early 2000's, so-called 'digicams' are having a resurgence. Maybe in the age of smartphone photography, imperfect photos are beginning to feel closer to the quality of memories. Small 'film-like' CCD sensors and on-camera flash imbue the photos with a nostalgic quality. A cherished example of mine is the Canon Ixus 70.
